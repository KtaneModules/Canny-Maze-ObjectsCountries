On the Subject of Canny Maze

The thing that gets me about these modules is that the instructions are actually well-written, even though we only see images and text. We create the whole solution in our text-editing software, and it turns out correct.

On this module will be one of the following images of Mr. Incredible becoming canny:

These are numbered 1-7, and are randomly distributed across a grid with dimensions each ranging from 5 to 8.

The number of rows is equal to (the number of batteries modulo 3)+5, and the number of columns is equal to (the number of ports modulo 3)+5. The current position's row is calculated by the third character of the serial number modulo the number of columns, and vice versa with the sixth character of the serial number.
